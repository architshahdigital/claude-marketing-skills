# Ad Copy Angle Templates

Fill the brackets. Keep one idea per ad; match the CTA to the funnel stage.

## 1. Problem–Agitate–Solve
> Still [painful workaround]? Every [time period] it costs you [tangible loss].
> [Product] [does the job] so you [desired outcome] — without [the pain].
> **CTA:** Learn more / Get started

## 2. Outcome / Benefit-led
> [Desired after-state] in [timeframe]. [Product] gives you [specific benefit], [second benefit], and [third].
> **CTA:** Shop now / Try it

## 3. Proof / Authority
> [Concrete result or number] — here's how. [Credible proof: data, review, credential]. [Product] made it repeatable.
> **CTA:** See the results

## 4. Contrarian / Pattern-break
> Everyone tells you to [common advice]. It's why [common failure]. Do [counterintuitive thing] instead — [why it works].
> **CTA:** Read why

## Headline patterns (test 3 per ad)
- Number-led: "3 ways to [outcome] without [pain]"
- Question: "What if [outcome] only took [effort]?"
- Direct benefit: "[Outcome], minus the [obstacle]"

## Reminders
- Primary text: hook in the first line (it's what shows before "…more").
- One clear CTA per ad. Test angles across ads, not inside one ad.
